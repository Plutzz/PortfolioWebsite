Rat's King

Unity 2D | 02/23 - 05/23

Description

On the streets of Victorian London, there remains a land of knights, chivalry, and the Rat Table. Join Billy, the Rat's bastard knight, as he journeys across the land in search of his destiny. Billy uses his sharp fountain pen tip to slash foes close to him. Striking enemies fill his fountain-pen spear with blood allowing for a ranged, special attack.

Contributions

  • Utilized advanced camera techniques to create a minimap that shows an abstract and zoomed out view of the world around the player.
  • Worked with a 2D artist to design pixel art sewer tiles and the main level of the game.
  • Implemented sound effects and music using Unity's built in audio system.
